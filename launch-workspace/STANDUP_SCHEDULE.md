## üìã STANDUP FORMAT

### 1. What did we accomplish yesterday? (3 min)
**Each person shares:**
- Completed tasks
- Key wins
- Progress made

### 2. What are we doing today? (3 min)
**Each person shares:**
- Planned tasks
- Goals for the day
- Dependencies

### 3. What blockers do we have? (5 min)
**Identify:**
- Blockers preventing progress
- Help needed
- Resource constraints
- Decisions needed

### 4. Any wins to celebrate? (2 min)
**Share:**
- Small wins
- Positive feedback
- Team achievements

### 5. Key metrics (if available) (2 min)
**Review:**
- Progress vs. targets
- Key numbers
- Status updates
