## ðŸ¤– AI Features

### AI Lead Scoring

**What it does:**
- Scores leads from 0-100
- Identifies hot leads automatically
- Routes leads to right sales rep

**How to use:**
1. Leads are automatically scored
2. View scores in Contacts list
3. Filter by score (e.g., "Hot Leads > 70")
4. Set up auto-routing rules

### Deal Closure Probability

**What it does:**
- Predicts likelihood of deal closing
- Shows risk factors
- Provides recommendations

**How to use:**
1. Open any deal
2. View "Closure Probability" widget
3. See risk factors and recommendations
4. Take action based on insights

### Predictive Analytics

**Features:**
- **Revenue Forecasting:** 90-day revenue predictions
- **Churn Risk:** Identify at-risk customers
- **Upsell Opportunities:** Find expansion opportunities
- **Scenario Planning:** What-if analysis

1. Go to Settings â†’ Automations
