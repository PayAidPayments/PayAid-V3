# GTM Launch Checklist

## Content deliverables

- Product onboarding tutorial(s)
- Product demo video
- Release notes / announcement blog post
- Sales deck + pricing sheet

## Sign-off flow

1. Draft owner assigned.
2. Stakeholder review complete.
3. Final assets exported and versioned.
4. Publish links recorded in launch artifact.

## Artifact location

- `docs/evidence/gtm/<YYYY-MM-DD>-launch-assets.json`

## Required fields

- `asset_type`
- `owner`
- `final_url_or_path`
- `approved_by`
- `approved_at`
